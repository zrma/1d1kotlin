Division

You need to read two large positive numbers a and b. Divide a by b and find the quotient q and the remainder r. Then, print the result in the following format: a = b * q + r


Sample Input:
5
3

Sample Output:
5 = 3 * 1 + 2


Sample Input:
10000
10

Sample Output:
10000 = 10 * 1000 + 0



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
