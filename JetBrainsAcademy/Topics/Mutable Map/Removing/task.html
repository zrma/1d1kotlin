Removing

Input: currentMap: MutableMap<Int, String>, value: String

Output: resulting MutableMap<Int, String>

Sample Input:

currentMap: 0 to "Sun", 3 to "Earth", 1 to "Mercury"

value: Sun

Sample Output:

{3=Earth, 1=Mercury}
