The roots of equation

There are numbers \(a, b, c, d\). Output in ascending order all the integers between \(0\) and \(1000\) (inclusively), which are the roots of the equation \(a \cdot x^3 + b \cdot x^2 + c \cdot x + d = 0\).

If a specified interval does not contain any roots of the equation, do not output anything.


Sample Input:
-1
1
-1
1

Sample Output:
1


Sample Input:
0
1
-6
5

Sample Output:
1
5
