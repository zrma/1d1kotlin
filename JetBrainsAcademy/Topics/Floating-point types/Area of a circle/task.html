Area of a circle

Write a program that reads a radius R and calculates the area of a circle with a given radius.

Use this formula: \(S = \pi R^2\).

Where \( S \) – an area of a circle, \( \pi \) is 3.1415, and \( R \) – the radius of the circle.

Input: one number of type Int.

Output: one real number.

Do not format or round your result. Use the given value of \(\pi\).


Sample Input:
25

Sample Output:
1963.4375



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
